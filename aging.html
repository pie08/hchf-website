Aging & Hormones

Aging is the decrease in anabolic hormones

Physiological changes from decreased hormone levels

Increases in

  • tightness/rigidity
  • body fat
  • body weight
  • obesity
  • bodily dysfunction
  • risk of cardiovascular disease
  • sedentary lifestyle
  • risk of high blood pressure
  • risk of type 2 diabetes

Decreases in

  • mobility/flexibility
  • muscle mass/muscle tone
  • strength
  • movement
  • overall health
  • quality of life
  • bone density

Key factors to offset the effects of aging

  • Strength training
  • Movement
  • Stretching

I provide my clients with personal care by designing & implementing specific exercise programs to help counteract the natural effects of aging and improving their quality of life.
